About Us - Pet Pantry

Welcome to Petco Feed!More Than Just Pet Food - A Nutrition Revolution.

Our mission is to provide our customers with an unparalleled pet feed for their pets, offering a curated selection of high-quality, affordable feed products. We believe that pet feed should be accessible to everyone, regardless of any region in any condition.

Brand Overview

Petco Feed is a global online pet feed brand that offers healthy, affordable, and high-quality pet feed.We started Petco Feed because we wanted to help. As well as donating a meal to those less fortunate each time a new dog signs up to Butternut, we also work with a number of charity partners each year. We're not saying that we're next in line for the Nobel Peace Prize or anything, but whether it's volunteering at our local foodbank or building new shelters for rescue dogs, we hope that the work we do helps a little bit.

Our Journey of Growth!

Petco Feed was founded in 2024 by Talha a vetenary expert who shared a passion for creating a platform that enables individuals tp provide healthy nutrition to their pets. Since our humble beginnings, we've grown into a thriving online community, with a loyal customer base and a reputation for excellence.
